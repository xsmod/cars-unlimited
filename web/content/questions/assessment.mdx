Use these questions to evaluate your organization's readiness for implementing a robust CI/CD pipeline.

## Application Architecture

- Is your application monolithic or microservices-based?
- What are your database migration strategies?
- Do you have stateless, horizontally scalable services?

## Requirements & Constraints

- What are your compliance and regulatory requirements? (SOC2, GDPR, etc.)
- Do you have existing Azure commitments or license agreements?
- What is your risk tolerance for deployment failures?

## Current State Assessment

- What is your existing CI/CD setup? (if any)
- What are your current deployment pain points?
- How is infrastructure currently managed?

## Team & Capacity

- How many engineers can dedicate time to this initiative?
- Do you have existing DevOps expertise in-house?

## Testing & Validation

- What automated testing currently exists?
- What are your SLA requirements?
- Do you have performance baselines?
