## Team Requirements

- **1 Platform Engineer** - Pipelines, infrastructure automation, tooling setup\
- **1-2 Backend/Full-stack Engineers** - Application CI/CD, testing frameworks, deployment testing
- **1 Security Engineer** (part-time) - Security scanning, compliance, secret management
- **1 Engineering Manager** - Project coordination, stakeholder communication

**Minimum Core Team:** 2-3 engineers with mix of DevOps and application expertise
